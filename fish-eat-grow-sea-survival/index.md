## Fish Eat & Grow: Sea Survival

**Effective date:** 2026-01-18

---

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game **“Fish Eat & Grow: Sea Survival”** (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information Collection and Use

The App does not require user registration and does not request personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through third-party services used by the App.

---

## Types of Data Collected

### Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

The App may collect the following types of non-personal information:

- **Device or other identifiers**
  - Advertising ID
  - App instance identifiers
- **App activity**
  - Gameplay interactions
  - Session duration
  - In-game events
- **Diagnostic information**
  - Crash reports
  - Performance data and error logs
- **Approximate location**
  - Country or region derived from IP address

The App does **not** collect precise location data such as GPS coordinates.

---

## Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

- To improve gameplay experience and app functionality
- To analyze app usage and performance
- To diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues
- To display advertisements, including rewarded advertisements
- To comply with legal and policy requirements

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

- **Google AdMob** – advertising services
- **Google Firebase Analytics** – analytics and performance tracking
- **Google Firebase Crashlytics** – crash reporting and diagnostics

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.  
The developer does not control how these services process user data.

---

## Data Sharing

User data may be shared with third-party services only as necessary to provide advertising, analytics, and app functionality.

The developer does **not** sell, trade, or rent personal data to third parties.

---

## Data Security

All data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS / TLS).

The developer does not store personal user data on private servers.

---

## Children’s Privacy

**Fish Eat & Grow: Sea Survival** is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## User Rights

Since the App does not require user accounts and does not store personal data directly, there is no user account data to access, modify, or delete.

Data collected through third-party services is processed according to their respective privacy policies.
